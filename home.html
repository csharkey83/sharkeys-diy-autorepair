ABOUT THE SITE

This website is intended to help all of you DIY auto repair guys (and gals) who are looking to save some money on labor cost. A lot of the work can be done my any body, however, it is IMPORTANT to know your abilities and seek professional help if it is beyond your capabilities.

In this web site, you will find links to helpful sites that have tips, how to, and diagnosing help for your car repair needs.

Visit the Interactive Garage to find information on a particular system.

The search engine on this site has been modified to help you find additional information on specific topic and search for car parts.
